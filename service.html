Services Provided

itter & Company, LLC clients range from those with simple individual tax returns and financial statement compilation, to complex corporate tax returns of companies with annual revenues in excess of $840 million and having over 2,000 employees.

SERVICES INCLUDE:

  1. Tax Services
  2. Accounting Services
  3. Auditing Services
  4. Business Consulting
  5. Litigation & Support Services
  6. Estate Planning

Other services provided are family wealth preservation planning and family business succession planning. These areas of expertise enable our clients the opportunity to plan now in ways that will benefit their families for future generations.
